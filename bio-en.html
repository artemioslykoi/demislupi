Demis Lupi

Artist trained at the Accademia di Belle Arti di Brera in Milan under the guidance of Alberto Garutti, Demis Lupi develops a pictorial language integrating traditional techniques with digital tools.

His artistic research explores human experience, relationships, and the most intimate folds of thought through a universal and spiritual archetypal language. Color plays a fundamental role, vital energy that identifies us and marks the cardinal points of being, an arcane bridge between the visible and the inexpressible, anchored to direct observation.

He avoids formal deformations preferring photographic crops and studied compositions, as evidenced by exhibitions Brand New Talent (Palazzina Liberty, Milan 2003), Ecodesign (Brera 2004), and the film Saveart: Recycling Art.

Continuous experimentation through painting and digital technologies.
